NATIONAL ANTHEM
Arise, O compatriots, Nigeria's call obey
To serve our fatherland
With love and strength and faith
The labour of our heroes past
Shall never be in vain
To serve with heart and might
One nation bound in freedom, peace and unity.

Oh God of creation, direct our noble cause
Guide our leader’s right
Help our youth the truth to know
In love and honesty to grow
And living just and true
Great lofty heights attain
To build a nation where peace and justice reign.

NATIONAL PLEDGE
I pledge to Nigeria my Country
To be faithful, loyal and honest
To serve Nigeria with all my strength
To defend her unity
And uphold her honour and glory
So help me God.

TOGETHER AS ONE 🇳🇬
A blessed nation clocks her birth.
A nation honored with tongues of different heritage
— Rolling off the tongue with beautiful voices;
Flowing like that of river Niger —
A nation where happiness shines,
as the stars twinkle in freedom.
Oh! watch the Niger area!
As she welcomes all into her blest home,
And embraces people into her faithful heart.
Oh! We've aged sixty!
Let the stars twinkle in joy!
As we celebrate,
Let the moon rise and the sun shine!
As we honor a nation,
Let the earth bow and the sky roar!
As the tide comes again,
Oh! I say, Let the water flow in praise!
As her breath comes easy,
Let the wind spread across her borders.
Oh! let me hymn to you,
Songs of a golden nation;
Precious land of my fathers before,
Where strength and unity reigns.
She's ageing in honor and glory
As happiness twinkle,
And the stars shine in freedom.
Oh! Rise to salute a nation,
In commemoration, we are!
Come all! Come one!,
You Giants of Africa!
Come join the celebration!
As I summon six brothers.
Come forward with one accord,
And display her solidarity as one.
Let the drums sing;
Dance with me in riant.
As a great Nation
Spread across fertile soils,
Enriched with rich minerals.
Her shield on her national flower;
Held as one by her dignity,
Is raised high up in soar
by a falcon uplifting her strength
Oh almighty!
Bless her from blues to blue
Keep soaring!
In unity and faith,
In peace and progress,
as one liberated nation;
A leader to many,
Guardian to thousands.
Sixty years in love and unity!
Shall not be spooled in diversity
For the toil of our Fathers
Shall not be made futile.
So help us, God!
